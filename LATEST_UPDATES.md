## 🎯 How to Use

### Voice Input
1. Click the **green microphone button** (🎤)
2. Wait for it to turn **red and pulse**
3. **Speak your question** clearly
4. Question is automatically sent when you stop

### Text-to-Speech
1. **Hover** over any bot message
2. **Click the speaker icon** (🔊) that appears
3. **Listen** to the response
4. **Click again** to stop

### Enhanced Questions
Try these new conversational queries:
- "Hello" or "Hi" - Get a friendly welcome
- "Help" - See what the bot can do
- "Give me a summary" - Get overview of all results
- "Thank you" - Receive acknowledgment
